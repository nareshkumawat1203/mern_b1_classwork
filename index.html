India

India, officially the Republic of India, is a country in South Asia. It is the seventh-largest country by area and the most populous democracy in the world.

It shares borders with Pakistan, China, Nepal, and others.

History

India has a long history, from the Indus Valley Civilization to the Mughal Empire and British colonial rule. India gained independence in 1947.
